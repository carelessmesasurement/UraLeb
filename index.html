History of UraLeb

UraLeb began as a small team of nuclear researchers in the mid-90s. Our goal is to investigate the safe use of uranium for energy production.

Research Areas

Our work at UraLeb covers several core areas in nuclear research:

  • Uranium Isotope Separation: Studying methods to separate uranium isotopes.
  • Safety Protocols: Researching the best practices for uranium handling.
  • Energy Development: Optimizing nuclear power use.
